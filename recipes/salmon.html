Lemon Panko Crusted Salmon

Delightful Salmon with a Crunch

Salmon is a wonderful and versatile fish. It can be cooked in many different ways; from pan-searing to baking to air frying. It can be cooked with or without a breading and seasoned with various spices and sauces to alter the taste. This salmon recipe in particular, coats the salmon in seasoned panko breadcrumb and bakes it in the oven to perfection.

Ingredient

  • Salmon
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Lemon pepper seasoning
  • Granulated Garlic
  • Dried Thyme
  • Lemon Zest
  • Melted Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil
  2. Combine panko bread crumbs, lemon pepper seasoning, granulated garlic, dried thyme, and lemon zest into a bowl.
  3. Brush salmon with melted butter
  4. Coat the salmon in the bread crumb mix and place on the baking sheet
  5. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until salmon is flakey

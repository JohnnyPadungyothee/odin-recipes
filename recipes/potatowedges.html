Potato Wedges

The French Fry's Cousin

Potato wedges are very similar to french fries as they are both finger foods made of potatoes and are primarily used as side dishes to accompany another dish. Potato wedges, however, are much "meatier" and keep more of the potato in comparison to the skinnier and crispier french fries.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Ground paprika
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degress F
  2. Cut potato into wedge shape
  3. cover the wedges in olive oil
  4. Season the wedges with paprika, salt, and pepper
  5. Place the wedges in the air fryer for 10 minutes
  6. Flip the wedges and air fry for another 5 minutes
